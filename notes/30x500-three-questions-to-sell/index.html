Lessons from 30x500: 6.Ask the right question: How do I make a product I know will sell?

This is a significantly better question than the usual "I made a product, how do I make it sell?"

It can be expanded into the following questions:

  1. What do my customers need?
  2. What do my customers want?
  3. What do my customers buy?
