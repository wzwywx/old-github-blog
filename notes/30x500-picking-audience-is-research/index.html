Lessons from 30x500: 7.Picking an audience is research, not competition

In research (perhaps pure research), the goal is to learn. Even if the findings don’t conform to what you wish it to be, it’s still a win. Apply the same mentality to picking an audience: our job is not to pick a winning audience, but to learn whether it is a winning one. Winning or not - we’ve learned something.
